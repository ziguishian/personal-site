Systems thinking is not about making the world more complicated. It is about seeing the structure behind complexity.

> Real growth is often not a single breakthrough, but the natural result of a system that keeps running.

![Systems thinking diagram](/images/thinking/system.svg)

## From Action To Structure

A single action solves one problem. A good system keeps lowering the chance that the same problem returns.
