## Context

Lingju Vision Mini Program deeply combines AIGC with customized e-commerce. It aims to turn user inspiration into generated, previewable, customizable and purchasable physical products.

## Process

I built the product loop around AI pattern generation, product preview, custom ordering and manufacturing delivery, using a mini program to lower the entry barrier.

## System

![Lingju Vision Mini Program visual](/images/works/shian-ai/cover.svg)

- AIGC Design for personalized patterns
- Product Mapping for real merchandise previews
- Custom Commerce from idea to order
- C2M Pipeline connecting inspiration with manufacturing

## Reflection

The future of AI commerce is not adding AI to product pages. It is making creation the starting point of transaction.
