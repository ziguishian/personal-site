## Context

Freeworkors was a freelance platform from my first startup period. It aimed to connect independent talent with real demand in a more flexible way.

## Process

The product was built as a mini program around freelancer profiles, demand posting, matching and communication conversion. I led product definition, design and development.

## Result

The platform launched publicly and received media coverage from Alibaba Cloud and NetEase.

## Reflection

Freeworkors taught me that platform products are not only about features. They are about creating trust and certainty on both sides of a market.
