## Context

This graduation project is a desktop robot designed for autism therapy assistance. It aims to support emotion recognition, language training and behavior guidance through gentle, repeatable interaction.

## Process

I explored therapy scenarios, children's interaction rhythm, hardware form and guided training flows, then shaped the robot's product and industrial design.

## Result

The project ranked first in my major and became the only graduation project rated excellent.

## Reflection

This project helped me think about technology, industrial design and real human needs together.
