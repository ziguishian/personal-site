## Context

Image generation should not be limited to typing prompts. It should feel closer to natural creation: speak the idea, adjust instantly, generate continuously and move closer step by step.

## System

![Voice-to-Image AI Agent visual](/images/works/compound-practice/cover.svg)

- Voice Input for natural creative intent
- Intent Parsing for style, subject, scene and constraints
- Prompt Builder for translating expression into generation prompts
- Generation Loop for iteration, selection and refinement

## Reflection

Good AI interaction does not ask people to learn machine language. It lets machines understand human expression.
